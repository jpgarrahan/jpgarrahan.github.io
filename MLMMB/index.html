Machine Learning meets
Many-Body Physics
University of Nottingham, UK, April 8-10, 2024

This workshop will bring together a small number of experts from across the physics/applied-maths/CS divide with common interests in machine learning theory and its applications in order to exchange ideas and identify synergies.

Invited speakers include:

Giulio Biroli (ENS Paris), Marin Bukov (MPI-PKS), Valentin De Bortoli (DeepMind), Arnaud Doucet (DeepMind/Oxford), Marylou Gabrié (Paris), Martin Gärttner (Jena), Thiago Guedes (Jülich), Markus Heyl (Augsburg), Zala Lenarčič (Ljubljana), Florian Marquardt (MPI Erlangen), Jannes Nys (EPFL), Nicola Pancotti (AWS), David Pfau (DeepMind), Frank Pollmann (TU Munich), Grant Rotskoff (Stanford), Adam Smith (Nottingham), Riccardo Zecchina (Milan)

Programme:

The workshop will start in the morning of Mon Apr 8 and finish by lunchtime Wed Apr 10 (full schedule TBA).
